Imran's Tortellini Recipe

Description

This recipe is an old favourite dish of mine as it was something I remixed once together during Ramadan. I had purchased the ingredients once from Morrisons and after a quick google search of recipes, this is what I was able to come up with

Ingredients

  • Three cheese Tortellini
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Italian herbs
  • Double cream
  • Chicken
  • flour
  • milk

Method

  1. Preheat some water and add a pinch of salt. Once it comes to a boil then pop in your tortellini
  2. Next we will prep our chicken, simply add some salt, pepper and paprika and shallow fry
  3. On a pan now heat some milk and add two tablespoons of flour and mix thoroughly
  4. Once the milk starts to mix with the flour it should become a thick sauce
  5. Add a teaspoon of italian mixed herbs
  6. then add your chicken to the thick sauce and add in your tortellini
  7. stir for 3-5 minutes and then add Mozarella cheese into your mix
  8. Now plate and serve your delicious easy food
